Stir Fry Vegetables

Description

This recipe will have you create the most flavourful vegetable stir fry right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of assorted vegetables (frozen or fresh)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbso corn starch
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp of any neutral cooking oil
  • Black pepper

    • Steps

    1. Pour 1 tbsp of cooking oil into a pan and set the heat to the highest setting
    2. As the pan heats, mix water, cornstarch, dark and light soy sauce, and oyster sauce in a small bowl
    3. Once the pan has heated, add all your vegetables to the pan and start stirring and tossing
    4. Once some fond has formed on the bottom of the pan, mix the sauce so the cornstarch can be reincorporated and pour into the pan
    5. Once a semi-thick sauce has formed, turn off the heat and add black pepper to taste.
    6. Done!
