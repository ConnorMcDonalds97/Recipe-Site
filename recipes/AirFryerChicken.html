Air Fryer Chicken

Description

This recipe produces amazing, juicy, perfectly seasoned chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • Club House Montreal Chicken Spice; or any seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breast with your seasoning of choice to your own tastes
  2. Line the air fryer basket with aluminum foil
  3. Set the air fryer to 375 degrees fahrenheit for 15 minutes
  4. Cook the chicken breast and flip halfway through
  5. Once the chicken has finished cooking, let rest for 15 minutes before eating
  6. Enjoy!
