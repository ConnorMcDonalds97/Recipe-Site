Steamed Rice

Description

Fool-proof recipe for classic steamed rice

Ingredients

  • Any kind of rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash rice with plain water by rinsing and stirring in water, then pouring out water when cloudy. Repeat until water runs clear after stirred.
  2. Add rice and water to rice cooker in 1:1 ratio. You can also guage the water level by placing your pointer finger on top of the rice surface, the water should barely reach the line of your first finger joint.
  3. Turn on rice cooker and wait until it finishes.
  4. Fluff up rice with scooper when done
  5. Enjoy!
